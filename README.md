# Samsung Supply Chain & Logistics Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Supply Chain & Logistics Analytics Dashboard developed for Samsung using Power BI.

The dashboard provides end-to-end visibility into the company's supply chain operations, covering suppliers, inventory management, production quality, shipment performance, and customer sales.

The objective is to support data-driven decision-making by monitoring key operational KPIs and identifying opportunities for optimization across the supply chain network.

---

## Business Problem

Managing a global supply chain requires continuous monitoring of suppliers, inventory levels, shipment performance, and customer demand.

Without a centralized analytics solution, decision-makers face challenges such as:

* Supplier delivery delays
* High inventory holding costs
* Product defects
* Shipment disruptions
* Revenue leakage and profit reduction

This dashboard addresses these challenges by providing a unified view of operational performance.
